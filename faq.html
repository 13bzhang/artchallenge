Frequently Asked Questions

What is the aRt Challenge?

The aRt Challenge is a game to solve a mystery about a stolen painting. Each level of the game can be solved used using R programming, the internet, and your brain.

What's the purpose of this game?

We like R and we like riddles. We hope you will learn something by solving riddles using R.

What is R?

According to Wikipedia: "R is a programming language and software environment for statistical computing and graphics. The R language is widely used among statisticians and data miners for developing statistical software and data analysis." According to our friend Steven, R gives us "mad skills."
