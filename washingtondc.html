Back in your Washington, D.C. office, you wait -- not so patiently -- for the next lead. You stayed in Paris for another three days to locate the source of the phone call; however, you were unsuccessful. Agent Lee has some suspicions about which criminal organizations are involved but those are merely suspicions.

"You'll never believe this!" Agent Lee bursts into your office. "Antique Road Show just called. They said a woman brought a painting in for appraisal. I think it's our painting."

But you are not so sure. The painting could be a forgery. So you request to subpoena the painting and order a chemical analysis of its paint.
