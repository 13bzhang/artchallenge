About the aRt Challenge

The aRt Challenge is a game to solve a mystery about a stolen painting. Each level of the game can be solved used using R programming, the internet, and your brain.

You can use any programming language to solve the riddles, but we recommend you try R. We think it'd be a good learning experience. Though you may be frustrated at times. Currently there are 8 levels to the game; more will be available soon.

Of course, we're big fans of notpron and the Python Challenge.
